About Us

What Do We Do?



Prime Property Dealer deal in purchase, sale, rent and lease of commercial and residential properties. Our area of expertise is from Bandra to Andheri. We deal in prelaunch, under-constructions, resale as well as ready to move new-construction projects. Our residential property portfolio ranges from Compact 1 Bedroom Apartments to Duplex Penthouses and Private Luxury Sea Facing Bungalows. Our commercial portfolio has more than 1,000,000 square feet of shops, showrooms and offices for sale and rent. We are the Go-To-Guys when you are looking to purchase or rent your commercial or residential property in Mumbai.

Prime Property Dealer is a 2nd Generation premium real estate investing and brokerage firm. We were established by the Late Mr. C. D. Kewalramani in 1988. We specialize in purchase, sale and lease of residential properties such as apartments, flats, single family homes, villas, bungalows, etc. in South Mumbai and Mumbai’s South Western Suburbs of Andheri to Bandra.We have been helping clients from all over the world in purchasing and renting their dream homes in the city of Mumbai. It is not an easy task to find the right home for your family in this densely populated city. We understand that and take the utmost care and effort in making sure that all your needs and requirements are taken care of. We believe in providing a professional and patient demeanor to our genuine clients.

We pride ourselves in our professionalism. Our biggest strength is providing results for our clients. We strongly believe in educating our customers about the dynamic and fluctuating Mumbai realty market. As your property consultant, we believe that it is our duty to maintain honest and straight forward communication.

Our expertise in purchase, sale or leasing in commercial properties is unparalleled. We have a range of commercial shops, commercial showroom and commercial office space in Mumbai’s growing Western Suburbs of Andheri to Bandra. We pay great attention to our corporate clients as well as the small business owner to cater to their business needs. As your realtor we understand the mantra of “Location Location Location”. We find it our duty and responsibility to understand your requirements thoroughly and educate you to the best of our knowledge in acquiring your business premises.

Over the years we have developed strong network which helps us to cater to everyone’s needs and specific requirements. It is through this wide and versatile network, we are able to cater to everyone’s diverse requirements. You can be rest assured that we will educate and guide you accordingly to fulfill your requirements.

Whether you are an owner who wants to sell or lease out his/her property or looking to buy, lease or invest in a property, we will be your one and only point of contact. Please feel free to contact us for all your property solutions.
